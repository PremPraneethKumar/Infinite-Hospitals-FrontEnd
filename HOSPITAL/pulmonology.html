PULMONOLOGY

    

    
OVERVIEW

      
In the modern world, breathing problems are becoming common for everyone irrespective of age. During the COVID crisis, people suffering from breathing problems reached an all-time high. The pulmonology department at Kamineni Hospitals provides diagnoses and treatment for a wide range of respiratory problems that affect the lungs, breathing, and sleeping disorder.
      

      
Pulmonology specialists deal with diseases involving the respiratory tract. It mainly deals with the lungs and other organs that help you breathe. It is related to intensive care medicine that involves diagnosing and treating patients suffering from respiratory disorders.

    
Kamineni Hospital being one of the best pulmonology hospitals deploys the latest technology and strategies in ventilator and respiratory care. We offer profound treatment for asthma, lung cancer, pulmonary tuberculosis, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), pneumonia, obstructive sleep apnoea, etc.

Pulmonologists are medically experienced in diagnosing and treating respiratory-related problems. The respiratory system mainly includes three parts the airway, lungs, and respiratory muscles.
